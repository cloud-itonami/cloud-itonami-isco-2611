## Customer

- individuals
- small businesses
- co-counsel firms

## Offer

- legal consultation
- document drafting
- court filing preparation
- case-file management

## Revenue

- hourly/flat-fee representation
- document preparation fee
- retainer subscription

## Trust Controls

- no filing or submission to a court/registry without governor gate
- conflict-of-interest check required before accepting new representation
- client privileged communications are auditable, not editable
